CHOOSE YOUR DOMAIN NAME

Not sure how to choose your domain name?   Probably it's best to keep it short, yet similar to your business name. It is a good idea to register several domains and associate them all with your website.

Once you decide which domain name to register or transfer, the next step is to configure functionality of your website.

Below you can select domain zones (max. 20) and check if your domain name is available.   Apart from the well-known zones (.com, .net, .org, etc.), there are hundreds of newer zones available.   Place the mouse pointer over the zone name to see the registration fee.

Some domain names might be taken in the old zones, but are available in the new ones!
