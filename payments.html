Online Payment System

The more payment options you provide to customers, the more sales you make. A web payment system allows easy online payments both for business owners and for the customers.

We adapt the code according to customers' requirements; design will be adapted too so that your new payment page will look similar to your existing website. If your existing website (e.g., yoursite.com) does not need corrections or redesign we may add an independent section (e.g., pay.yoursite.com) to it for payment processing.

A typical online payment procedure works as follows. A customer receives a bill with a four symbol order ID. (S)he enters this ID on https://pay.yoursite.com where the bill is paid with a credit card, see images below.
